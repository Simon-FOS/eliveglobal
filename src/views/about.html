Our Story

Elive Global Consult & Construction is a premier construction and consultancy firm based in Jos, Plateau State, Nigeria. With our RC Number: 7266342, we are committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake.

Founded with a vision to transform the construction landscape in Nigeria, we have grown to become a trusted partner for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the region.

Our team of experienced professionals combines technical expertise with innovative solutions to deliver projects that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.
